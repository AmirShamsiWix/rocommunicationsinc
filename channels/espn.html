Frequently asked questions

How do I get ESPN on DIRECTV via Satellite?

To watch DIRECTV via Satellite ESPN channels, all you need is one of the following TV packages: ENTERTAINMENT and above, MAS ULTRA, or LO MAXIMO.

What channel is ESPN on DIRECTV via Satellite?

You can watch ESPN on channels 206 and 1206 with DIRECTV via Satellite.

What channel is ESPN Plus on DIRECTV via Satellite?

ESPN Plus isn’t a DIRECTV via Satellite channel. You can subscribe to ESPN Plus via the ESPN App.
