Frequently asked questions

Where do I return DIRECTV equipment?

Whether you’re replacing your DIRECTV HD DVR or cancelling your service, returning DIRECTV equipment is easy. Make sure you have your account number handy, and go to your local FedEx or UPS store. You won’t need a box or label. If the equipment isn’t returned after 21 days of cancellation or change, an equipment charge will apply.

Does DIRECTV charge for equipment?

Every DIRECTV package includes one free Gemini™ device. DIRECTV via Satellite includes standard professional installation, while DIRECTV via Internet is self-installed. If you add services, you may incur additional charges.

What is a DIRECTV Gemini™?

The DIRECTV Gemini™ is a wireless receiver that offers an enhanced watching experience, plus a voice activated remote control powered by Google Assistant. The Gemini™ provides easy access to thousands of streaming apps**, as well as SignalSaver™ technology** that protects your connection from outside interruption.

**High-speed Internet connection required. Separate subscription/Login required for 3rd party app content. Google login required.

**Requires HD DVR (model HR44 or later) connected to high-speed internet. SignalSaver only in case of satellite signal outage. Available on 160+ most-watched channels (based on DIRECTV viewership).

Where can I buy DIRECTV equipment?

If you’re a new customer looking to purchase a DIRECTV package or a current customer wanting to upgrade to the latest DIRECTV equipment, call 1.888.271.5839.

What equipment is needed for DIRECTV via Satellite?

All you need to start watching DIRECTV via Satellite is a satellite dish, a receiver, and a remote.

Does DIRECTV have a wireless receiver?

Yes! The Gemini™ receiver connects wirelessly to your device.
