Frequently asked questions

What should I do if a DIRECTV app isn’t working?

If your DIRECTV App is not working, call 1.888.653.2917 to speak with a technical support expert.

How does the DIRECTV App work?

The DIRECTV App paired with a supported device (along with your DIRECTV username and password) will let you take all your favorite programming on the go. Just download the app, sign in and enjoy!

How do I download the DIRECTV App?

To download the DIRECTV App, simply visit directv.com/download on your mobile device, or go to your app store of choice and search for “DIRECTV.” From there, just open the app and sign in with your DIRECTV ID and password.
